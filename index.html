Updated Guide for Choosing Sonic's Refreshing New Summer 2024 Drink Menu

As the summer heat starts to set in, Sonic Drive-In is unveiling a brand new lineup of refreshing and creative beverage offerings for 2024. The fast-food chain is introducing its Sonic Drinks Menu also know as "Flavorista Favorites" which is a collection of six exclusive drink options that will only be available through the Sonic mobile app.

What makes Sonic's menu items stand out compared to other fast food chains

Introducing the Flavorista Favorites

The Flavorista Favorites menu features a diverse array of flavors and textures, perfect for quenching your thirst on a hot summer day. Let's take a closer look at the new drink options

Classic Cruiser

This drink combines the classic taste of cola with hints of cherry and vanilla, creating a delightfully sweet and nostalgic flavor profile.

Twisted Flamingo

For a more tropical twist, the Twisted Flamingo blends lemon-lime, cherry vanilla, and sweet cream into a refreshing slush. Paradise Sunset Bursting with citrus and berry flavors, the Paradise Sunset features a vibrant mix of blood orange and real strawberry chunks.

Rainbow Slush

This colorful frozen concoction is a blend of blue raspberry, real strawberries, and lemonade, resulting in a visually stunning and flavorful slush.

Lemonade Cream Cooler

Sonic's classic lemonade is transformed into a creamy, dreamy slush in this delightful summertime treat.

Grape Escape

For a bubbly and unique option, the Grape Escape combines the sweet taste of grape with a hint of lime.

Exclusive App-Only Offerings

The new Flavorista Favorites menu will be available exclusively through the Sonic mobile app starting on July 1, 2024. This means customers will need to download the app and place their orders digitally to access these unique drink options. Sonic is debuting the Flavorista Favorites at the X Games Ventura 2024 on June 28, giving attendees a sneak peek before the nationwide rollout. After that, the new drinks will be available for ordering through the app across all Sonic locations.

Customizing Your Sonic Drinks

In addition to the Flavorista Favorites, Sonic's regular drink menu offers a wide range of customization options. Customers can add real fruit, candy, and other mix-ins to their slushes, shakes, and other beverages to create their own unique flavor combinations.

Customizing Your Sonic Drinks

In addition to the Flavorista Favorites, Sonic's regular drink menu offers a wide range of customization options. Customers can add real fruit, candy, and other mix-ins to their slushes, shakes, and other beverages to create their own unique flavor combinations.

  • Adding vanilla or cream to slushes
  • Mixing different fruit flavors like strawberry, mango, and peach
  • Blending in candy pieces like Reese's, M&M's, or Oreo
  • Combining iced tea and lemonade for a "half and half" drink
